FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Biswap is a decentralized exchange built on BSC that lets users swap tokens with a 0.1% fee. It supports cross-chain swaps through Wormhole and Squid Router across 30+ chains, and uses a V3 AMM for flexible liquidity provisioning and better capital efficiency.

Biswap charges 0.1% per swap — among the lowest on BSC. Up to 90% of these fees are returned through fee mining, where traders receive BSW tokens proportional to their trading activity.

Biswap supports cross-chain swaps across 30+ blockchains including Ethereum, Polygon, Avalanche, Fantom, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Solana, and more through Wormhole and Squid Router integrations.

BSW is Biswap's governance and utility token with a 700M supply. It's used for voting, earning rewards from fee mining and liquidity pools, and participating in the 3-tier referral system.

Biswap is non-custodial — users retain control of their funds at all times. Smart contracts have been audited, and the platform operates on BSC's proven infrastructure.

Biswap has a 3-tier referral system where you earn commissions from people you refer, their referrals, and one level deeper. Rewards come from swaps, farms, and pools — distributed in real-time to your wallet.
